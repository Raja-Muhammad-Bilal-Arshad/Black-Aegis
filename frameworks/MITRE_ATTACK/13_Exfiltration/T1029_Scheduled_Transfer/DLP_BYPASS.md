# DLP Bypass Guide: Scheduled Transfer (T1029)

## The Human Blindspot
SOC analysts work shifts, but "Off-Hours" (Weekends, Holidays, 3 AM) often have reduced staffing or rely purely on automated alerts.

## Evasion Techniques
1.  **DLP Maintenance Windows**: Attackers look for times when security tools specific updates or reboots occur.
2.  **Noise**: Exfiltrating during "Backup Windows" (e.g., Friday 8 PM) allows the traffic to blend in with massive legit data flows.
